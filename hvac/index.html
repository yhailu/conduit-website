Your receptionist can't answer 3 calls at once. She doesn't work at 11pm on a Saturday. And she takes vacation. HeroCall handles overflow, after-hours, and peak season surges — the calls your receptionist physically can't get to. Think of it as backup that never calls in sick.

48 hours from sign-up to live. We'll hop on a 20-minute call to learn your services, service area, and scheduling preferences. We build your custom AI agent overnight, you test it the next day, and you're live.

Plans start at $199/month. That's less than one missed job. No setup fees, no per-minute charges, no long-term contracts. Most contractors see ROI within the first week.

AI triages urgency automatically. No heat in January? That gets flagged as emergency and your on-call tech gets an immediate text + call. Noisy ductwork? That gets scheduled for the next available slot. You set the rules for what counts as urgent.

Yes. HeroCall works with any phone system — landline, VoIP, cell phone, Google Voice, you name it. Just set up call forwarding (takes 5 minutes) and you're good. Works with ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, and most field service software too.
