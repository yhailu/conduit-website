AI triages urgency based on weather, household factors (elderly, infants), and system type. Dispatches the right tech with the right skill set — boiler vs. heat pump vs. furnace.

Automatically enroll customers in seasonal tune-up plans. AI schedules spring AC checks and fall furnace inspections — filling your calendar before the rush.
💰 Estimate & Replacement Follow-Up
Customer got a quote for a new system but hasn't decided? AI follows up with personalized reminders about rebates, financing options, and seasonal pricing.

No more answering service fees. AI handles overnight and weekend calls, dispatches on-call techs for true emergencies, and schedules non-urgent requests for the next business day.
